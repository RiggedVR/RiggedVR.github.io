Rigged Games

Virtual Reality Game Development

Rigged Games is just Rigged's games. Rigged (Jake Phillips) is a VR Indie Game Developer, creator of Web-Guy and Untangled!


Rigged has been creating games since he was 10 years old, starting on Roblox and moving to Unity soon after. Rigged is now 18 and currently focused on Untangled and learning Unreal Engine.


Recently, with the public release of Untangled, Rigged has started working alongside some friends to speed up the creation and up the quality of the games!
