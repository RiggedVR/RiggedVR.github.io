Privacy Policy for Untangled VR Game

Effective Date: 21st December 2024

We at Rigged Games (“Rigged Games,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and shared when you access and use our virtual reality game, Untangled (the "Game"), and any related services (collectively referred to as the "Services"). This policy specifically relates to Untangled and any associated game services, including logging in via PlayFab, interactions within the game, and the use of third-party features like QuestCam.

By using the Game and the Services, you agree to the collection and use of your information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

In order to provide you with the best experience when using Untangled, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Meta Username: When you use Untangled, we collect your Meta username. This information helps us identify and authenticate users, personalize your experience, and link you to your account.
  • IP Address: We collect your IP address for PlayFab, a third-party service that manages certain aspects of the Game's functionality, including authentication and logging. This information is used to ensure secure and reliable access to the Game and to maintain server integrity.
  • Hardware ID: When logging into PlayFab, we collect your device's unique hardware ID. This information is used solely for authentication purposes and to ensure that your gameplay data is tied to your specific device, improving game security and performance.
  • QuestCam Data: If you use QuestCam features in the Game, QuestCam collects anonymized analytics data, such as timestamps for recording start and end times. This data cannot identify you and is used solely to improve QuestCam’s functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to provide, maintain, and improve the Game and related Services, including:

  • User Authentication: To authenticate you and allow you to log in securely using PlayFab.
  • Game Functionality: To operate and manage the Game and any related features, such as in-game progress tracking and achievements.
  • QuestCam Functionality: To enable recording and sharing features provided by QuestCam while analyzing anonymized data to enhance user experience.
  • Security: To monitor for security threats, unauthorized access, and protect your account from malicious activities.
  • Improvements and Analytics: To analyze usage trends and improve the overall performance of the Game and Services.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We share information with third-party service providers, such as PlayFab and QuestCam, to manage game authentication, data storage, and other functionalities necessary for the Game's operation. These providers are bound by contract to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or if we believe it is necessary to protect our rights, enforce our terms, prevent fraud, or comply with legal processes, we may disclose your personal information.

QuestCam Privacy Practices

QuestCam, integrated into the Game, is a service provided by intermeta, Inc. It collects and processes data as follows:

  • Data Collection: QuestCam does not collect any personally identifiable information. It only collects anonymized data, such as timestamps for the start and end of recordings, to improve app functionality.
  • Data Usage: The anonymized data is used to enhance QuestCam’s recording features, monitor trends, and ensure the smooth operation of the app.
  • Data Security: QuestCam implements security measures to protect anonymized data. However, no internet transmission or storage system can guarantee complete security.
  • User Rights: While QuestCam does not collect personal data, users may contact QuestCam at info@lmnl.org to inquire about data practices or request data deletion.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please note that we do not handle the storage of your personal data directly. Data collected from the Game, such as your Meta Username, IP Address, and Hardware ID, is stored and managed by PlayFab, while anonymized data from QuestCam is managed by intermeta, Inc.

Both PlayFab and QuestCam implement security measures to safeguard your data. For more information, please review the privacy policies of these services:

  • PlayFab Privacy Policy
  • QuestCam Privacy Policy (via contact email)

Data Retention and Deletion Rights

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with the Services or as required by law. Anonymized data collected by QuestCam is retained indefinitely unless deletion is requested.

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data at any time. To request deletion, contact us at riggedingame@gmail.com, and we will take steps to delete the data held by us and our service providers, including PlayFab and QuestCam.
